Profile

Auraspan exists for people who believe that how you carry yourself changes everything.

Founded by commercial model and coach Zach Ekeke, Auraspan combines the disciplines of physique, movement, appearance and personal standards into one philosophy for modern self-development.

Working with brands including GQ Germany, Financial Times, New Era and Schweppes showed that confidence isn't something you're born with — it's something you build through consistent habits, attention to detail and high standards.

Today, Auraspan turns those principles into coaching, experiences and resources that help people improve how they look, move, perform and present themselves.

Because becoming your best self isn't a destination.
It's a practice.

"Your journey doesn't have to start perfectly. It just has to start. And when you do start — commit to becoming someone your future self will thank you for."

— Zach Ekeke, Founder of Auraspan
